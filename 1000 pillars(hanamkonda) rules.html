Rules To Follow

1. Devotees are required to wear traditional attire.

2. Mobile phones and cameras are strictly prohibited inside the temple premises.

3. Do not carry any leather items inside the temple.

4. Smoking and consumption of alcohol are strictly prohibited.

5. Devotees should maintain silence and refrain from making loud noises.

6. Respect the sanctity of the temple and follow the instructions of the temple authorities.

7. Do not litter inside the temple premises. Use designated bins for disposal.

8. Keep your belongings safe and secure. The temple authorities are not responsible for any loss or theft.

9. Do not engage in any form of misconduct or unruly behavior.

10. Seek permission before offering any special prayers or performing rituals.

11. Observe the designated timings for darshan and avoid overcrowding.

12. Refrain from touching the idols and religious artifacts inside the temple.

13. Respect the queue system and wait patiently for your turn during darshan.

14. Do not bring any outside food or beverages inside the temple premises.

15. Participate in the rituals and prayers with devotion and reverence.

16. Ensure that children are accompanied by adults and supervised at all times.
